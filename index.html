Aidan Villasis

is a student at Springside Chestnut Hill Academy with a lifelong fascination for both technology and visual arts. The merger of these interests is reflected in the portfolios and other works showcased on this hub:



Mobile Photography

Aidan's interest in photography was sparked in 2018 after taking an introductory photography class. Since then, he has continued developing a photography portfolio throughout high school. Under the guidance of photographer Pete Capano, Aidan has developed a unique photographic style and continues to strengthen his skills. These pictures, all of which were shot on mobile phones, are the culmination of his work.

Lung Cancer PSA

Aidan was able to combine his interests in art, computers, and biology through designing a website to serve as a PSA and help educate people about lung cancer.

Creative Writing

With over 12 years of English writing experience (from Kindergarten until present) and over three years of high school writing, Aidan has developed and fine-tuned his creative style. The works shown are an assortment of creative, narrative, and analytical writings from 12th grade.

Villustrators

As a way to give back to the community during COVID, Aidan and his brother started a sign-making business with proceeds donated to Face to Face, a local charity that provides many services to community members in need. Aidan also designed and manages the website.
